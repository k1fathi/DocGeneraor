Detailed Explanation of Features

Awards Section

This section organizes all your achievements in a visual format, showcasing the cups you have received.

Cups

The cups are color-coded and represent different levels or types of awards. The colors correspond to the ranking:

  • Orange Cup: Participation or basic achievement.
  • Gold Cup: Intermediate level or milestone achievement.
  • Blue Cup: Advanced level or significant recognition.
  • White Cup: Top-tier or special achievement.

Use Case Scenario

In a typical interaction:

1. Access the Awards section of the interface.
2. Review the awards displayed as cups.
3. If you receive a new award, click on the button to include it.
4. To manage awards, simply use the delete icon next to any unwanted cups.
