User Guide for Manual Instagram Post (Secure)

Main Components

  • Data Source: Dropdown menu for selecting the data input source.
  • Image: File upload feature for selecting a JPEG/JPG image.
  • Caption: Text box for writing the post's caption.
  • Hashtag: Input for entering hashtags, separated by commas.
  • Publish Button: Button to submit the post to Instagram.

Usage Instructions

Steps to Create a Manual Instagram Post:
  • Select a data source from the dropdown.
  • Click on "Choose File" to upload your image.
  • Enter the caption you would like to use for your post.
  • Type in the relevant hashtags, separating them with commas.
  • Click the button to submit your post.

Detailed Explanation of Features

  • Data Source: This field lets you select where the input data is sourced from, essential for managing multiple inputs.
  • Image: It is crucial that the selected file is in JPEG or JPG format to be accepted by the system.
  • Caption: Captions should be concise and relevant to engage your audience effectively.
  • Hashtag: Including hashtags increases visibility; ensure they are related to your content.
  • Publish Button: Upon clicking, your image, caption, and hashtags are processed and posted on Instagram.
