Trend Analysis User Guide

Main Components

  • Trend Analysis Graph: Displays the trend over time with data points representing various dates.
  • Tooltips: Show additional information on specific dates when hovered over.
  • Date Markers: Indicate specific dates and corresponding trend values.
  • Navigation Options: Access additional functionality such as zooming or exporting data.

Usage Instructions

To use the Trend Analysis tool, follow these steps:
  • Navigate to the Analytics section of the application.
  • Select Trend Analysis from the list of options.
  • Observe the graphical representation of the trends.
  • Hover over any point on the graph for detailed information.
  • Select buttons to zoom in or export the data if needed.

Detailed Explanation of Features

  • Trend Graph: Represents data points with a smooth line, highlighting the overall trend.
  • Data Point Hover: When hovering over a data point, a tooltip appears showing the exact date and the trend value.

Use Case Scenario

A user wants to analyze the engagement trend over a week:

  • They access the Trend Analysis tool from the Analytics section.
  • They observe the upward and downward trends indicated on the graph.
  • Upon hovering over the graph, they discover a peak engagement on June 2, 2023.
  • They decide to export this data for further reporting.
