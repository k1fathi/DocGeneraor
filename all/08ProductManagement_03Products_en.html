Product Management User Guide

Main Components

  • Product List: Displays all products with details including ID, name, category, and more.
  • Export Options: Export product data to Excel.
  • Search Bar: Allows users to search for specific products.
  • Action Buttons: Provide options for editing or deleting product entries.

Usage Instructions

  • To search for a product, enter the product name in the search bar and press Enter.
  • To export the product list, click the "Export Excel" button.
  • To edit a product, click the edit icon next to the product entry.
  • To delete a product, click the trash icon next to the product entry.

Detailed Explanation of Features

Export to Excel: This feature allows users to download the current product list as an Excel file for further analysis.

  • Search Functionality: Improves efficiency by allowing users to quickly find products using specific keywords.
  • Product Actions: Direct actions are available next to each product for easy management.

Use Case Scenario

A user wants to update the information for a sports shoe listed in the product management interface. They can quickly locate the product using the search bar, click the edit icon to modify the details, and then save changes. If they wish to share this information with colleagues, they can simply click the “Export Excel” button to download the updated product list.
