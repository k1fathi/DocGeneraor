User Guide for Most Dashboard

Main Components

  • Total Followers: Displays the total number of Instagram followers.
  • Total Following Users: Indicates the number of accounts being followed.
  • Engagement Metrics: Includes various graphs showing engagement statistics.
  • Demographics: Provides insights into follower demographics represented in a pie chart.

Usage Instructions

Follow these steps to use the Most Dashboard effectively:

  • Log in to your account.
  • Navigate to the Reporting section.
  • Select the Most option to view your statistics.

Detailed Explanation of Features

  • Total Followers: Shows the cumulative number of followers over time.
  • Post Count Graph: A line graph displaying the number of posts over a selected period.
  • Comment Count: A visual representation of the number of comments received, allowing for analysis.
  • Engagement/Post: Tracks the average engagement on each post, useful for assessing content performance.
  • Peak Activity Times: Analyzes the times when user interactions are highest.
  • Demographic Information: Insights into gender distribution among followers illustrated with a pie chart.

Use Case Scenario

Consider a user named Alex who manages an Instagram account:

  • Alex logs in and navigates to the Most Dashboard.
  • He reviews the Total Followers section, noticing it has increased to 1000.
  • Alex looks at the Engagement Metrics to analyze what content types lead to higher interactions.
  • Finally, he examines the demographic pie chart to tailor his upcoming posts to his audience.
