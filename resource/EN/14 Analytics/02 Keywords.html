Analytics Dashboard User Guide

Main Components

  • Search Results Graph: Displays the number of search results over time for two different sites.
  • Instagram Hashtag Engagement: Shows the engagement metrics for various hashtags over several months.
  • Related Hashtags Overview: Compares the performance of related hashtags across different months.

Usage Instructions

To effectively use the analytics dashboard, follow these steps:

  • Access the dashboard by navigating to the Analytics section.
  • Select the "Keywords" tab to view the relevant graphs and metrics.
  • Hover over the graphs to view detailed data points for specific months.

Detailed Explanation of Features

  • Search Results Graph:
    Displays monthly data of searches made on both sites.
  • Instagram Hashtag Engagement:
    Illustrates the engagement metrics per hashtag over selected months.
  • Related Hashtags Overview:
    Compares counts of various related hashtags to identify trends.

Use Case Scenario

A typical user may want to analyze the effectiveness of their marketing strategies:

1. Open the analytics dashboard.

2. Review the Search Results Graph and compare the performance of Site1 and Site2.

3. Analyze Instagram Hashtag Engagement to adjust social media strategies accordingly.

4. Use the Related Hashtags Overview to discover which additional hashtags to utilize for better reach.
