Global Trends User Guide

Main Components

  • Television Trends: Most searched television brands and their popularity.
  • Moisturizing Creams: Most searched moisturizing creams.
  • Sports Shoes: Popular searches related to sports footwear.
  • Chocolate Trends: Popular chocolate brands and products.

Usage Instructions

To use the Global Trends interface:
  • Navigate to the desired category: Television, Moisturizing Cream, Sports Shoes, or Chocolate.
  • View the top 10 trending items in each category.
  • Interact with the interface by clicking on items for more details.

Detailed Explanation of Features

  • Real-Time Data: The trends are updated periodically to reflect current searches.
  • Ranking System: Items are ranked based on their popularity metrics.
  • User Interaction: Users can click on items to receive more information.

Use Case Scenario

Suppose a user is interested in purchasing a television. They can:
  1. Access the "Television" section to view trending brands.
  2. Take note of the most searched terms, such as “lg television” and "vestel."
  3. Click on a specific brand for additional product details or links to purchase.
